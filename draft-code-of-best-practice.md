## Context

Under **section 31 of the Gambling Regulation Act 2024**, the GRAI has the power to make Codes of Practice. The GRAI's Guidance on Relevant Obligations for Business to Consumer Licensees (17 February 2026) notes that:

> "The guidance in this document (as may be amended) may in the future be contained in a Code of Practice made under section 31 of the Act."

This Draft Code of Best Practice (v2.3) is likely an early draft of such a Code of Practice, setting out standards expected of gambling licensees in Ireland. The document may cover topics such as:

- Responsible gambling measures
- Player protection standards
- Advertising standards
- Operational requirements for licensees
- Staff training and conduct
- Complaints handling
