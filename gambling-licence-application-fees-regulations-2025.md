## Betting Licence Application Fees

### In-person Betting Licences (s.4)

**(1) Relevant Premises (on-course bookmaking)**

The fee is based on turnover from in-person betting activities at relevant premises in the applicant's last financial year. For applicants with no prior in-person betting activity in Ireland, the estimated turnover for the first 12 months following licence grant is used.

**See:** [Table A](#table-a--in-person-betting-relevant-premises) in Schedule 1.

**(2) Other Premises (off-course bookmaking)**

The fee is based on the same turnover calculation as above, **plus an additional fee of €1,200 for each premises** from which the licensed activity will be provided.

**See:** [Table B](#table-b--in-person-betting-other-premises) in Schedule 1.

**(3) Combined On-course and Off-course**

Where betting is to be provided from both a relevant premises and other premises, the fees from Table A and Table B are both payable and are added together.

### Remote Betting Licence (s.4(2))

The fee is based on turnover from remote betting activities in the applicant's last financial year (or estimated turnover for first 12 months for new operators).

**See:** [Table C](#table-c--remote-betting-licence) in Schedule 1.

### Remote Betting Intermediary Licence (s.4(3))

The fee is based on turnover (for intermediaries: the total amount paid in charges for providing the betting facility) in the applicant's last financial year (or estimated turnover for first 12 months for new operators).

**See:** [Table D](#table-d--remote-betting-intermediary-licence) in Schedule 1.

## Gaming Licence Application Fees

### In-person Gaming Licence (s.5(1))

The fee is based on turnover from in-person gaming activities in the applicant's last financial year, **plus an additional fee of €1,200 for each premises** from which games will be provided.

**See:** [Table A](#table-a--in-person-gaming-licence) in Schedule 2.

### Remote Gaming Licence (s.5(2))

The fee is based on turnover from remote gaming activities in the applicant's last financial year (or estimated turnover for first 12 months for new operators).

**See:** [Table B](#table-b--remote-gaming-licence) in Schedule 2.
